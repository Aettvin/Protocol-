# Ættvin Protocol

> **The open standard for personal AI.**

Ættvin Protocol is an open-source standard that allows your personal AI to communicate securely across your home, your car, your hotel room, and beyond — without ever compromising your privacy.

## ️ The Philosophy

We believe personal AI should belong to *you*, not to the cloud. 

- **Privacy-First:** Your data never leaves your control. No hidden agendas, no data selling.
- **Local-First:** AI runs on your devices. The cloud is optional, never mandatory.
- **Interoperable:** Your AI at home should understand your AI on the road.
- **Transparent:** Open-source, auditable, and built on trust.

## 🏗️ What is Ættvin?

Ættvin (Old Norse for "Family Friend") is an ecosystem of personal AI applications built on a shared, open protocol:

- **Ættvin Heim:** Your family's AI at home.
- **Ættvin Gest:** The guest's AI companion in hospitality.
- **Ættvin Hlíf:** Aging in place, safely and independently.
- **Ættvin Protocol:** The open standard that connects them all.
